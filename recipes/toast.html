Toast

Description

Who doesn't like a good piece of toast?

This classic food goes well with coffee, tea, and is at home on almost any plate of food

Ingredients

  • Sliced Bread
  • Butter
  • A toaster or oven

Steps

If using a toaster:

  1. Place the bread in the toaster
  2. Push the lever down on the toaster
  3. When the bread is done, butter to taste and enjoy!

If using an oven:

  1. Set your oven to 200 degrees Farenheit
  2. Once heated, place your bread in the oven for 5 minutes
  3. Flip your bread and leave for another 5 minutes
  4. Remove from oven, butter to taste, and enjoy!
