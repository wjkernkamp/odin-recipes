Ramen

Description

A simple bowl of ramen makes a good snack on a cold day.

This ramen recipe adds an egg and some other ingredients to make it more meal worthy.

Ingredients

  • one pack of instant ramen noodles
  • one egg
  • green onions

Steps

  1. First boil some water.
  2. Then add the egg to the boiling water for exactly seven minutes.
  3. Remove the egg and add it to a bowl of cold water (ice water is best)
  4. Add the ramen to the pot of boiling water along with the seasoning
  5. Chop the green onions and add them to the ramen
  6. Stir
  7. Peel the egg and add it to the ramen, then carefully cut it in half
  8. Enjoy!
